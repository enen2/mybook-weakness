How to change
In our society, where there are many social norms
and strong expectations for members to uphold these practices,
people are expected to follow established traditions
and behave in ways that align with societal expectations.
Deviating from these expectations is often discouraged.
How to Address your Weaknesses?
Adopt a growth mindset, believing your abilities can evolve.
Break goals into small steps to reduce stress and boost action.
Use timely feedback to make practice more effective.
